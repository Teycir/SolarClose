☀️ SolarClose Calculation Methodology

Transparent, Conservative, and Industry-Standard

Overview

SolarClose uses realistic, conservative calculations to provide accurate solar savings projections. All formulas are based on industry standards and account for real-world efficiency losses.

Annual Solar Production

Formula:

Annual Production (kWh) = System Size (kW) × Sun Hours/Day × 365 × Derate Factor

Parameters:

  • System Size: Rated capacity in kilowatts (kW)
  • Sun Hours/Day: Peak sun hours for your location (default: 5 hours)
  • Derate Factor: 0.80 (80% efficiency accounting for losses)
Why 80% Derate? This accounts for:
  • Inverter efficiency losses (~4-8%)
  • Temperature effects (~3-5%)
  • Shading and soiling (~2-5%)
  • Wiring losses (~2-3%)
  • Age degradation buffer

Panel Degradation

Year N Production = Base Production × (1 - 0.005)^N

Solar panels degrade at approximately 0.5% per year. This is a conservative industry-standard rate.

Utility Bill Projection

Without Solar:

Year N Bill = Current Monthly Bill × 12 × (1 + Inflation Rate)^N

With Solar:

Year N Bill = (Current Monthly Bill - Solar Offset) × 12 × (1 + Inflation Rate)^N

Default Inflation Rate: 4% annually (customizable)

Solar Offset Calculation

Monthly Solar Offset = (Annual Production / 12) × Electricity Rate

This represents the monthly dollar value of solar energy produced.

Loan Payment Calculation

Monthly Payment = P × [r(1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n - 1] Where: - P = Principal (System Cost - Down Payment) - r = Monthly Interest Rate (APR / 12) - n = Number of Payments (Loan Term × 12)

Total System Cost

Net Cost = System Cost - Federal Tax Credit - State Incentives + Dealer Fee
  • Federal Tax Credit: Typically 30% (customizable)
  • State Incentives: Dollar amount (varies by location)
  • Dealer Fee: Optional financing fee

Maintenance & Replacement Costs

Annual Maintenance:

Optional $150/year (customizable) for cleaning, inspections, and minor repairs.

Inverter Replacement:

  • Standard Warranty: Replacements at Year 12 and Year 24
  • 25-Year Warranty: No replacement costs
  • Default Cost: $2,000 per replacement (customizable)

25-Year Savings Calculation

Total Savings = Σ(Year 1 to 25) [ (Utility Bill Without Solar - Utility Bill With Solar) - Loan Payment - Maintenance - Inverter Replacements ]

Break-Even Point (ROI)

The break-even year is when cumulative savings equal the net system cost. This is calculated by summing annual savings until the total exceeds the initial investment.

Environmental Impact

CO₂ Offset:

CO₂ Offset (lbs) = Annual Production (kWh) × 1.56 lbs/kWh × Years

Based on EPA's average grid emissions factor.

Trees Planted Equivalent:

Trees Equivalent = CO₂ Offset (lbs) / 48 lbs/tree/year

One tree absorbs approximately 48 pounds of CO₂ per year.

Conservative Assumptions

SolarClose uses conservative estimates to ensure realistic projections:

  • 80% system efficiency (accounts for all real-world losses)
  • 0.5% annual panel degradation
  • 4% utility rate inflation (historical average)
  • Maintenance costs included
  • Inverter replacement costs factored in
  • No optimistic "best case" scenarios
Note: Actual results may vary based on location, weather patterns, system orientation, shading, and utility rate changes. These calculations provide a realistic baseline for decision-making.
