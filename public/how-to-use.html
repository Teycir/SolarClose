Getting Started

SolarClose is designed to be intuitive and fast. Here's everything you need to know to start closing deals.

1Create a New Lead

Click the ‚ûï New Lead button at the top of the page. This creates a fresh lead with empty fields ready for your customer's information.

2Enter System Details

Scroll down to the Calculator section and fill in:

  • System Size (kW): The solar system capacity
  • System Cost ($): Total price before incentives
  • Current Monthly Bill ($): Customer's average electric bill
  • Electricity Rate ($/kWh): Their utility rate (default: $0.13)
  • Sun Hours/Day: Peak sun hours for location (default: 5)

3Configure Financing (Optional)

If the customer is financing:

  • Select Loan as financing option
  • Enter loan term (years), APR, and down payment
  • Add dealer fee if applicable

The calculator automatically shows:

  • Product Description: System details for the proposal
  • Proposal Conditions: Terms and conditions

Click üíæ Save Lead to store the customer in your local database. The lead will appear in your leads list with a timestamp.

Troubleshooting

Data Not Saving?

Make sure your browser allows local storage. Check browser settings and ensure you're not in private/incognito mode.

PDF Not Generating?

Ensure all required fields are filled (marked with red borders). The app will show which fields are missing.

Lost Your Data?

If you cleared browser data, your leads are gone unless you have a backup. Always export backups regularly!
